    Lab task 1 : Identify and evaluate websites

    I choosed a website about the same topic than me shequal.com To view the website correctly, here is a screen recording.

    The audience

    You can clearly see on the home page what the website is about, in other words, equality in advertising. The representation of the "She" suggests that this site is aimed more at women. The aim of the site seems to be to educate people, as you can read on the web page where the collective's mission is clearly described: "It’s our mission to raise industry and public awareness, reinforce positive behaviours, and empower people to take action in shaping how women are represented in the stories we tell and consume." However, it is difficult to know what type of company this is. Especially when you look at the home page, which looks like a blog. The aim is to arouse curiosity, so there's a tab where you can contact the site's author for more information.

    Affordances, conceptual models and signifiers

  • As I said earlier, the aim of the site seems to be to create curiosity but also to educate people about the importance of equal advertising. You can see that there are different tabs, which makes it easy to navigate and see the problem being highlighted as well as the actions being taken.
  • The site is easy to understand, but nevertheless seems a little slow and the home page gives the impression of being a catch-all.
  • The use of images is particularly important because the site deals with advertising. So there is a concrete illustration. In addition, the home page tries to highlight the brand's products. There is an audio podcast and an illustration of the book.

    • Contrast, repetition, alignement, proximity

    The site seems to follow a clear graphic charter. However, there are two orange bars that are constant and can affect the user experience. The strong presence of coloured bands can make the titles a little blurred, but they are written in a large enough font to stand out.

    Content design

    The website deals with a specific subject and seems to present a relevant angle for the consumer. The content appears to be in-depth, particularly when you consider the book featured. However, the sources used are not highlighted. The site seems to emphasise a personal point of view.
